Welcome to Gadget Byte

Back in 2010, there were no websites or blogs that used to give information about gadgets available in Nepal. And this is how I got an idea to start blogging about gadgets available in Nepal. I created a blog called Gadgetsinnepal.blogspot.com in 2010, where I shared the prices and specifications of smartphones available in Nepal. Than I created a facebook fan page to interact with my readers and solve their questions and queries. And thanks to you all, with all your love and support, we were able to launch this website to give you more information of tech world. Hope you all will enjoy reading our post.
